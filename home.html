WELCOME TO OUR RESTAURANT

RESTAURANT

The place that Italian cuisine enthusiasts describe as the best Italian restaurant in Marrakech offers an indulgent interlude orchestrated by Chef Massimiliano Alajmo. A true culinary institution in the ochre city, Le Palais des Saveurs has joined the closed circle of the best restaurants in the region in the World’s 50 Best Restaurants. Guests or visitors alike: get ready to experience the quintessence of Italian gastronomy.

BAR

Our spacious and newly refurbished bar is the perfect place to unwind after a long day. Enjoy a wide menu of wines, ranging from France to as far as Australia, or indulge on an array of cocktails prepared by our bartenders using the freshest ingredients with the best liquors. We have also available a selection of small plates and light meals ideal for a quick lunch or to accompany your beverage.

RESTAURANT HISTORY

The origins of Italian food can be traced all the way to ancient Rome - both the cuisine and the spirit, art, and love of eating. Surprisingly, Italian eateries have been around for centuries, since long before they were even known as "eateries”, or trattorias. These establishments began popping up throughout Italy, most likely in the early Middle Ages. They quickly became popular amongst the travelers.
